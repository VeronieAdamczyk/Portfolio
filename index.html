Experience & Education

Just It - Developers Professional Program

August 2018 - Present

Currently I am participating in the Developers Professional Program ran by Just IT. This is an intensive, self-funded course that teaches the fundamentals of web design for both front end and back end development.

The fundamentals of this course allows the class to work on further projects outside of the classroom environment. By working on our own projects, it helps to develop our skills as web developers with more complexity.

Completing the training in class of a small number of students means that the learning environment was filled with students with the same interest and passion for web development. This also allowed us to help each other when needed to help us solidify our own learning.

Homebase - Visual Merchandiser

June 2016 - August 2018

My role as a Visual Merchandiser allowed me to be creative. I worked with the Buying and Merchandising team to develop layouts for the Homebase estate. This included working with key suppliers to make sure that everyone involved were happy with the finished result. I would live build the layouts, where necessary, then transfer them to planograms on the computer so that stores were able to follow the layouts when implementing.

During this period we opened 24 store conversions. This would mean travelling to these stores during the merchandising phase to make sure that layouts worked and had the right flow for the customer and if needed to change layouts and update these layouts so that previous stores were able to update to the most current layout.

This role gave me the opportunity to develop on my leadership skills as my line manager would give me leave in her absence. This included attending meetings and training new members of the team.

Homebase - Assistant Merchandiser

March 2016 - June 2016

In this role I worked with the Merchandiser to maintain the Security and Electrical category. I would mainly make sure that orders were placed weekly for products of low availability. I would also communicate with suppliers to check that they had the products available themselves and update the orders accordingly.

Although I was only in this role for a brief period it taught me further how to communicate and work with people cross functionally.

Lindt & Sprungli - Business Development Manager

July 2014 - March 2016

In this role I was responsible for the upkeep of the brand within the grocery format. I would visit stores to ensure that products were displayed to the agreed plan and making sure that the brand was throughout the year round, especially during peak times e.g Christmas and Easter.

A large part of the role was selling displays into stores and maintaining them by ordering stock into the store to keep the display replenished and sellable during the year round promotional periods.

As I would travel around to stores throughout the day, this job taught me how to work autonomously as well as part of a team by keeping in contact with other members of the team via phone or traveling to help them if they needed physical assistance.
